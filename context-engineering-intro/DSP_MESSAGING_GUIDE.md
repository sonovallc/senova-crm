# Senova CRM DSP Messaging Guide

## Executive Summary
This guide provides comprehensive messaging strategy for Senova CRM's Demand Side Platform (DSP) capabilities, specifically tailored for medical aesthetics practices including med spas, dermatology clinics, and plastic surgery practices.

---

### 1. **Enterprise-Level Advertising at Practice-Friendly Pricing**
**Core Message:** "Get the same advertising power that Fortune 500 companies use – without the $50K minimum spend"
- Wholesale pricing through our own DSP
- No agency markups or hidden fees
- Start with budgets that make sense for your practice

**Core Message:** "Know exactly where your ads appear – and where they don't"
- Choose specific websites, apps, and platforms
- Block competitors' sites and inappropriate content

### 3. **Precision Targeting That Fills Your Schedule**
**Core Message:** "Reach the exact patients you want, when they're ready to book"

- 60B+ behaviors tracked weekly
- Target by demographics, interests, location, and intent

### 4. **Platform Independence & Higher Approval Rates**
**Core Message:** "Break free from Meta and Google's restrictions"
- Own DSP means better campaign approval rates
- No more rejected ads for aesthetic procedures
- Diversify beyond walled gardens that control your reach

### 5. **Data-Driven ROI You Can See**
**Core Message:** "Track every dollar from ad to appointment"
- Real-time campaign performance data
- Full transparency on where budget is spent
- Direct attribution to booked appointments

## Implementation Notes

1. **Lead with benefits, support with features** - Practice owners care about results, not technology
2. **Use concrete examples** - "$5,000 agency fee vs. $0 with direct access"
3. **Address compliance concerns** - Medical practices need reassurance about advertising compliance
4. **Focus on control** - Practice owners want to feel in charge of their marketing
5. **Emphasize transparency** - Show how they can see exactly where money goes
6. **Compare to their current reality** - Most know agencies/platforms, show the difference

## Final Recommendations

1. **Always lead with cost savings** - This is the most immediate, tangible benefit
2. **Quickly address the "complexity concern"** - Make it clear this is easy to use
3. **Use medical-specific examples** - Botox, fillers, laser treatments resonate
4. **Include competitive comparisons** - Especially vs. agencies and Facebook/Google
5. **Provide clear next steps** - Make it easy to see the platform in action

This messaging positions Senova CRM's DSP as the intelligent choice for medical practices that want professional results without enterprise costs or agency dependencies.
