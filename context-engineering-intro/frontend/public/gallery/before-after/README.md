# Before/After Gallery Images

This directory contains before and after photos for the gallery section.

## Required Images

You need to add actual client before/after photos (with permission) for the following services:

### Microblading
- `microblading-before.jpg` - Close-up of eyebrows before microblading
- `microblading-after.jpg` - Close-up of eyebrows after microblading

### Lip Blushing
- `lips-before.jpg` - Close-up of lips before treatment
- `lips-after.jpg` - Close-up of lips after lip blushing

### HydraFacial
- `hydrafacial-before.jpg` - Face before HydraFacial treatment
- `hydrafacial-after.jpg` - Face after HydraFacial treatment

### Permanent Eyeliner
- `eyeliner-before.jpg` - Eyes before permanent eyeliner
- `eyeliner-after.jpg` - Eyes after permanent eyeliner

### Body Contouring
- `body-before.jpg` - Body area before Venus Bliss treatment
- `body-after.jpg` - Body area after treatment showing inch loss

### Lash Lift
- `lash-before.jpg` - Eyes/lashes before lash lift
- `lash-after.jpg` - Eyes/lashes after lash lift

## Image Requirements

- **Resolution:** Minimum 600x600px, ideal 1200x1200px
- **Format:** JPG (compressed for web)
- **Lighting:** Consistent lighting between before/after
- **Angle:** Same angle and distance for before/after pairs
- **Consent:** Written consent from clients for photo usage
- **Quality:** Professional quality, in-focus, clear details

## Temporary Placeholders

Until real photos are available, the gallery will show image placeholder errors.
Consider using professional before/after photos from:
1. Your own client portfolio (with signed consent)
2. Stock photo websites with beauty/cosmetic procedure images
3. Supplier-provided before/after examples (if licensed for use)

**IMPORTANT:** Never use fake before/after images or misleading photos. This can damage trust and may violate advertising regulations for medical aesthetics.
