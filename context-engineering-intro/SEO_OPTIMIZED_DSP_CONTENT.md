## Content Implementation Notes

1. **Keyword Density**: Maintain 1-2% keyword density for primary keywords
2. **Readability**: Keep sentences under 20 words where possible
3. **Headers**: Use H2 and H3 tags with keywords naturally incorporated
4. **Lists**: Use bullet points for scanability and featured snippets
5. **CTAs**: Include clear calls-to-action every 2-3 sections
6. **Schema Markup**: Implement SoftwareApplication and Organization schema
7. **Alt Text**: Include keywords in image alt text naturally
8. **URL Structure**: Ensure URLs include primary keywords (already optimized)
