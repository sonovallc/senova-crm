## Simple Explanations for Complex Features

### "How Does It Know Who to Target?"
Think of it like this: Just like Netflix knows what shows you might like based on what you've watched, we know who might want your product based on what websites they visit, what they search for, and what they buy online.

### "Where Do My Ads Show Up?"
Your ads can appear:
- On news websites and blogs
- In mobile apps and games
- On streaming services (like Hulu or Spotify)
- Before YouTube videos
- On social media platforms
- Pretty much anywhere people spend time online

### "How Is This Different From Facebook Ads?"
- Facebook ads only show on Facebook and Instagram
- Google ads only show on Google and partner sites
- Senova ads can show EVERYWHERE online
- Plus, you pay wholesale prices instead of retail

### "Why Is It Cheaper?"
Imagine buying groceries:
- Agencies = Shopping at a fancy grocery store with huge markups
- Senova = Shopping at a wholesale club where you buy direct
Same products, much better prices.

## Implementation Notes

### When Writing Copy:
1. **Lead with the benefit**, not the feature
2. **Use examples** from everyday life
3. **Avoid "marketing speak"** - write like you're explaining to a friend
4. **Focus on results**, not process
5. **Keep sentences short** - if you need a comma, consider two sentences

### Remember:
The goal isn't to sound sophisticated or technical. The goal is to help business owners understand how Senova can help them get more customers without the confusion, complexity, or high costs they're used to.
